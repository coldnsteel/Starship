# Operator Handout — Phase-1

## Your role
Guide, witness, recorder, and safety authority.

## What you must do every session
- Run the pre-session checklist  
- Stay within arm’s reach of the EMERGENCY STOP  
- Observe continuously  
- Record abnormalities immediately  
- Debrief participant calmly  

## What not to do
- Don’t multitask  
- Don’t run sessions alone  
- Don’t ignore gut sense

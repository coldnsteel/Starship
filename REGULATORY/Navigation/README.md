# REGULATORY — Navigation Hub

## Purpose
Regulatory contains pathways, checklists, and templates required for investigational device usage, veterinary approvals, plant/food safety consultations, and future marketing claims.

## Contents (high level)
- `fda-pathway.md` — Notes on 510(k), investigational device exemptions, documentation checklist
- `veterinary-clearance.md` — Veterinary approval pathways and templates
- `organic-certification.md` — Guidance for agricultural claims and certification considerations
- `ethics-irb-template.md` — IRB-ready protocol template and consent forms
- `data-privacy.md` — PHI handling, anonymization, and GDPR notes

## Quick links
- **IRB template**: use this to start human pilot submissions.
- **Veterinary**: required for companion and livestock pilots.
- **Organic/food safety**: consult before labeling food products.

## Notes
- For initial pilots operate as "investigational adjunct devices" with IRB oversight.
- Track regulatory interactions and store correspondence under `REGULATORY/logs/`.
